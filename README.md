# 💫 About Me:
Akash Kolte is a software and product development engineer with over 4+ years of industry experience, currently pursuing an MS in Computer Science with a focus on AI and Machine Learning at the University at Buffalo (SUNY). He has a strong foundation in building scalable, production-grade systems and has worked extensively across the full stack, including backend microservices, distributed systems, cloud infrastructure, and modern frontend frameworks. In his previous role at Phenom People, he contributed to high-impact enterprise products, developing platforms and tools that improved onboarding efficiency, reduced operational overhead, and supported large-scale customer-facing systems.<br><br>Professionally, Akash is transitioning toward AI/ML-focused engineering roles while maintaining a strong systems mindset. His interests lie in applied machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, and large language model–based applications, with an emphasis on how these models are trained, deployed, and integrated into real-world products. Rather than focusing solely on theory, he prioritizes practical, end-to-end AI systems that combine robust software engineering with intelligent models.<br><br>In the short term, Akash aims to secure a full-time SDE or AI/ML Engineer role in the US, ideally within a strong product-driven or AI-first technology company, where he can work on scalable systems and production ML pipelines.
